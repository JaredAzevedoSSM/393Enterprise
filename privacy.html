Our Privacy Policy

We strive to be transparent about our data collection and usage practices.

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

393 Enterprise (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to keeping your data use minimal, transparent, and limited to what is necessary for our services to function.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use our website, products, or services (“Services”), how it is used, and your rights.

1. Our Core Principle

We do not collect or use your data beyond what is necessary for the functionality of the application.

  • Minimize data collection
  • Avoid unnecessary tracking
  • Be transparent about third-party involvement

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide
  • Name
  • Email address
  • Messages submitted through contact forms
  • Any information you choose to share when using our Services
b. Automatically Collected Information

Depending on the product or feature, limited technical data may be collected automatically, such as:

  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Basic usage data (e.g., page visits, interactions)

This data is used strictly to:

  • Ensure the service works properly
  • Improve performance and reliability

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information only to:

  • Provide and maintain our Services
  • Respond to inquiries or support requests
  • Improve functionality and user experience

We do not:

  • Sell your personal data
  • Use your data for unrelated marketing purposes
  • Collect more data than necessary for functionality

4. Third-Party Services

Some features of our Services may rely on third-party providers (for example: hosting platforms, analytics tools, or form processing services).

These third parties:

  • May collect or process data independently
  • Operate under their own privacy policies
  • Are not fully controlled by us

While we aim to choose privacy-conscious providers, we cannot guarantee or control how third parties handle your data.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purpose it was collected for
  • Comply with legal obligations (if applicable)

If data is no longer needed, we make reasonable efforts to delete or anonymize it.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Misuse
  • Disclosure

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data, including:

  • Accessing the data we have about you
  • Requesting correction or deletion
  • Withdrawing consent (where applicable)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • The “Last Updated” date will be revised
  • Significant changes may be communicated through our website
