Our Story

What 393 Enterprise Means

Our story is, in many ways, my story—so let’s start there.

393 Enterprise is currently a one-person operation. I’m an independent developer building projects driven by curiosity, problem-solving, and a genuine interest in creating things that are both useful and enjoyable. Most of what I build starts as a personal idea or challenge, and over time evolves into something I hope others can benefit from as well.

My approach is simple: build thoughtfully, keep things practical, and continuously improve. I value clean design, straightforward functionality, and keeping things as minimal and efficient as possible—both in how products are built and how they handle user data.

If you’re interested in my background or past work, you can explore more on my personal site. It provides a deeper look into my experience, projects, and the path that led to 393 Enterprise.

As this grows, so will the ideas behind it. Feedback plays a big role in that process. If you’ve used one of the products, have suggestions, or just want to connect, I’d genuinely like to hear from you.

Have feedback on a product? Thoughts on this site? Or an idea you think is worth building? Feel free to reach out—I'm always open to improving what exists and exploring what could come next.

My Mission

At 393 Enterprise, the goal is simple: build useful, reliable, and thoughtfully designed software without unnecessary complexity.

Every project is approached with a focus on clarity, performance, and real-world usefulness. Rather than chasing trends or overbuilding features, the priority is creating tools that solve problems in a straightforward and effective way.

A core part of this philosophy is respect for users—both in how products function and how data is handled. That means minimizing data collection, avoiding unnecessary tracking, and being transparent about how things work.

As 393 Enterprise grows, this mindset remains constant: build with intention, improve continuously, and prioritize quality over quantity.
