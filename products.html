The Products

These our the products that we currently offer and support.

Gicard

Gicard is a work in progress app that helps business and users track and use gift cards. Essentially a digital gift card platform, Gicard is meant for small businesses to easily create, sell, and monitor gift cards for their customers. It also helps users track all of their gift cards in a centralized location and avoid losing them or forgetting about them.

The Giver

The Giver is a native website and iOS app (not natively designed for iOS) that helps users track gift ideas for their friends and family. It also helps users keep track of important dates and events and a history of gifts given on past dates and events. The idea is a simple one: worry more about what to give and less about remembering who, when, and what to give (has a feature to suggest gift ideas too)!

Roadtripper

Roadtripper was our first project and is a website that coincedentally happened to exist already by the exact same name. While not as feature rich as the original, our version is a simple road trip planner that helps users quickly find new and interesting places to stop. Motivated by a lack of options on Apple Maps, Roadtripper is meant to help people explore places they might not have otherwise heard of.

Winnie

Winnie is a website that acts as a fitness companion for its users. Relative to existing sites that try to do the same thing, Winnie takes a simple approach and focuses more on the physical aspects and dietary guidance rather than trying to number crunch every last workout and meal. It also has a limited game aspect to help motivate users to stay on track and have some fun while doing it!

Website
Release Date

Budget Room

Budget Room is a simple website that at first glance just looks like a calendar. In reality, it is a quick budget planner to help people keep track of their finances and plan for future expenses (namely ones that are regularly occurring). Its features are limited, but it is straight to the point and helps you visualize your budget.
